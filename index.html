About Us

FIRE ENGINEERING

Fire Engineering is a powerful tool in the hands of those qualified to use it. It can be employed not only for the Building Owners/Developer but for the Property Development Industry as a whole.

INDUSTRY EXPERTS

Building Code Consultants is a dedicated Fire Protection Consultancy with more than 42 years of experience to draw on. We have at our fingertips the knowledge and expertise to interpret relevant rules, regulations and by-laws in the mass of sometimes confusing information, regarding installation codes of practice and other ambiguous regulations laid down by various Authorities.

RESPONSIBILITY

As the Fire Protection Consultants, it would be our responsibility to ensure that all emergency services are designed and installed to meet the design criteria of the approved Rational Fire Design, for which our practice would be held liable in the event of loss of life due to professional incompetence in a fire condition.

EXPERIENCED

Building Code Consultants has participated in and witnessed many significant events within the Fire Protection Industry. The launch of our practice was our first statement of consequence. Building Code Consultants entered the market with a lucid mission and genuine purpose. Today we can say with pride that we have achieved far more than we could possibly have hoped to attain.

QUALITY

Our accomplishments are mainly due to the quality of engineers employed and our close association with our sister company, Bramley and Associates who are well know throughout the industry as leaders in the Fire Protection Consultancy Field.

FUTURE THINKING

From a Company perspective, we have remained conscious of the fact that to maintain pre-eminence in our industry, we have to stay up-to-date with the latest world technological development thus helping us to sustain our position as leaders in the Fire Protection Industry and it would be our intention to draw on all aspects of good Fire Protection Design placing both Life Safety Aspects and economic viability at the forefront.

CERTIFICATION

It is our intention on all contracts, to ensure that a Fire Clearance Certificate is issued in accordance with occupation dates and ultimately ensuring that the building complies with Section “T” of the SANS 10400 National Building Regulations which may be accompanies with a Rational Fire Design.

Services

Our Practice, S.A.N.S., C.S.I.R. and A.S.I.B. approved, provides two types of Services, that being the Specialist Fire Protection and Fixed Fire Protection Services.

SPECIALIST FIRE PROTECTION ENGINEERING

1

Section T1(2)a of The National Building Regulations permits Rational Design Motivations to be submitted should the nature of the development be in contravention with S.A.B.S. 0400 Deemed-to-Satisfy Regulations thus permitting Architects to exercise their skills, expertise and allow the developer to erect an economically sound and safe building.

2

Having the necessary experience in the specialist field, we have the expertise to deal with fire related issues presented when adopting the Deemed-to-Satisfy regulations and in fact in many instances our fees are paid by Rationalisation. So we can, in a true sense offer you a "Free Service".

3

Below is a list of where savings are generally saved:

  • Number of Emergency Escapes
  • Travel Distances
  • Compartmentalization
  • Divisional Separation
  • Smoke Ventilation/Extraction
  • Smoke Detection Systems
  • Sprinkler Reticulations
  • Hydrant and Hose Reel Reticulations
  • Structural Fire Ratings
  • Water Supplies for Fixed Fire Protection Services
  • Fire Pumps and Water Storage Tanks

4

Building Code Consultants cc has a good working relationship with the S.A.B.S., C.S.I.R., A.S.I.B. and Local Authorities, and any design discrepancy can be resolved in consultation with the above parties.

5

Scope of Work which will apply to this Service:

  • Appraisal of Sketch Drawings to determine whether or not it would be cost effective to comply with the National Building Regulations or utilize the option provided by Rational Design.
  • To assist the Project Architects at sketch plan stage in the overall fire safety design concepts with emphasis on the principle item being emergency escape provisions.
  • Determine the overall fire safety design criteria in order to assist the Professional Team in the preparation of the relevant documentation and interfacing of services.
  • Prepare Fire Plans for submission to the City Council.
  • Prepare Innovative Rational Designs.
  • Attendance at Design meetings.
  • Site inspections and preparation of Inspection Reports.
  • Final preparation of Deviation Plans.
  • Final inspection with Fire Department and assist in obtaining the occupancy certificate.

FIXED FIRE PROTECTION SERVICES

1

Having over 42 years’ experience in Fixed Fire Protection, we have the ability to produce accurate designs that conform to any of the Acknowledged Rules and Regulations worldwide and to meet Rational Designs prepared by our Engineers. We provide detailed plan layouts together with Specification for Tender purposes and prepare co-ordinated Installation Drawings. This Service automatically eliminates abnormalities and variations.

2

During the Building Contract period, our Practice provides an Engineer who will attend Co-Ordination and Technical Meetings and act as the Clients/Architects Agent liaising between the Professional Team and the Fixed Fire Services Sub-Contractor, advising and approving Drawings as required and checking the Quality of Workmanship, thus ensuring efficient installation procedures with minimum problems. Our in-depth knowledge of the Fire Protection Industry, its Rules and Governing Regulations makes us ideally qualified to arbitrate should Contractual problems arise.

3

The following Scope of Work will apply to this Service (Annexure A Fire Consulting Engineer Services

  • Preparation of the overall Design Concept in respect of the Fire Protection Services required for the project, thus determining at an early stage the requirements in terms of the respective Fixed Fire Protection Services for the project.
  • Preparation of Tender documents.
  • Fire Services Budgets.
  • Assistance in the co-ordination of all Fire Services.
  • Attendance of Design and Site Meetings.
  • Commissioning and Handover of Fire Protection of Equipment.

4

Once we have an understanding of the Architectural Sketch Drawings, we will issue under separate cover our Proposed Design Philosophy and associated Recommendations.

TECHNICAL SET UP

Site should the Professional Team decide to follow this route, our C.A.D. and Electronic Mail System are compatible with all C.A.D. programmes.

Our drawing office is equipped with a high-resolution colour output plotters linked to state of the art C.A.D. stations running Auto C.A.D. 2016.

Having all the necessary tools and systems in place, the essential up-to-date information will be readily available facilitating efficient, hands-on management and control by the Project Associate.
